About OptiCrop

Opti Crop is an intelligent agricultural recommendation system that leverages machine learning and data science to help farmers make informed decisions about crop selection. Our system analyzes environmental factors, soil properties, and historical crop data to provide personalized crop recommendations.

Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with data-driven insights that optimize agricultural production, improve resource efficiency, and promote sustainable farming practices. By providing intelligent crop recommendations, Opti Crop helps farmers maximize their yields and minimize resource wastage.

How It Works

Opti Crop uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on over 2,200 crop samples to analyze your soil and environmental conditions. Our model considers seven critical factors:

Nitrogen (N) Essential for plant growth and protein synthesis
Phosphorus (P) Critical for root development and energy transfer
Potassium (K) Improves plant strength and disease resistance
Temperature Affects crop growth rate and maturity
Humidity Influences disease susceptibility and photosynthesis
pH Level Determines nutrient availability in soil
Rainfall Critical for crop water requirements
